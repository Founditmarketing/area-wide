Common Questions

Answers before you call

How much does asphalt driveway paving cost?
Residential asphalt driveways typically cost $3–$7 per square foot depending on thickness, sub-base prep, and site access. A standard 600 sq ft driveway runs $1,800–$4,200. We provide free itemized quotes within 24 hours — no hidden fees, no padding.
How long does a new asphalt driveway last?
A properly installed asphalt driveway lasts 15–20 years in East Texas with regular sealcoating every 3–5 years. Our hot-mix formulations are engineered for the region's clay soil and summer heat.
How long does it take to pave a driveway?
Most residential driveways are completed in 1–2 days. You can drive on the surface within 24–48 hours depending on weather conditions.
Do you remove the old driveway?
Yes. We handle full tear-out, grading, sub-base preparation, and new asphalt installation. Everything is included in the quote — no surprise line items.
