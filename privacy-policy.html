Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Area Wide Paving ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website areawidepaving.net. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

When you contact us for a quote or service inquiry, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name

We use the information we collect solely to:

Children's Privacy
