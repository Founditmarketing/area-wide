How much does an asphalt driveway cost in Northeast Texas?
Asphalt driveways in Northeast Texas typically run $3 to $7 per square foot for a properly installed surface over a compacted 6-inch crushed stone base. A standard 600–800 sq ft residential driveway costs between $2,400 and $5,600 depending on soil conditions, site access, and sub-base requirements. Paul provides an itemized written quote inside 24 hours of the site visit.
ii.
How long does an asphalt driveway last in East Texas?
Properly installed asphalt driveways in East Texas last 15 to 20 years with regular maintenance. Sealcoating every 3 to 5 years protects the surface from UV damage, oil staining, and the freeze-thaw cycles common in the region. The difference between a 5-year driveway and a 20-year one is almost always sub-base preparation, not the asphalt itself.
iii.
How long does the installation take?
Most residential driveways are completed in 2 days — one day for sub-base prep and grading, one day for asphalt placement and compaction. The surface needs 24 to 72 hours to cure before vehicle traffic, depending on temperature. We schedule around your work week.
